The Event

The National 3 is a world-class tournament designed to identify the greatest par 3 golfer in the world. Open to both professionals and amateurs through exclusive invitations and qualifying rounds, this event highlights the unique joy and strategic intensity of the par 3 experience on a global stage.

On September 24th and 25th, 2026, the inaugural championship will take place at the Mountain Par 3 Course at The Preserve Sporting Club in Wyoming, Rhode Island. Over two days and 36 holes, competitors will navigate a course defined by its stunning vistas and tactical complexity, providing an intimate and thrilling experience for players and spectators alike.

The elite field features 72 of the most accomplished golfers in the country. This group brings together proven professionals and top-tier amateurs who have demonstrated exceptional skill at the national and regional levels.
